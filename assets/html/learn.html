What Is Recycling?

Recycling is the process of gathering and processing items that would otherwise be discarded as waste and converting them into new products. Your community and the environment will both gain from recycling.

What's The Point?

Recycling accomplishes the goal of keeping materials out of landfills by converting them back into raw materials that may be utilized to create new products or items.

What Are Some Benefits

Benefits of recycling include

  • The amount of waste transferred to landfills and incinerators is reduced.
  • Natural resources such as timber, water, and minerals are conserved.
  • Increases economic security by utilizing a home material source.
  • Reduces the need to obtain additional raw materials, which reduces pollution.
  • It conserves energy.

    • These are all things that will help the world and bring us forward.
